## Piratenkönigin Scylla

*Medium humanoid (human), neutral evil* · **CR 9 (5.000 XP)**

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 18 (Studded Leather + Parry) | 143 (22d8+44) | 30 ft. (Phase 2: +Fly 30 ft.) |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 14 (+2) | 20 (+5) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 18 (+4) |

**Saving Throws:** Dex +8, Wis +4, Cha +7 · **Skills:** Acrobatics +8, Intimidation +7, Perception +4
**Senses:** passive Perception 14 · **Languages:** Common, Thieves' Cant

**Legendary Resistance (2/Day).** Can succeed on failed save.
**Phase 2: Elaras Erwachen.** At ≤71 HP: gains fly 30 ft., weapon attacks deal +7 (2d6) radiant.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Three Shadow-Poison Scimitar attacks.
**Shadow-Poison Scimitar.** *Melee +8, reach 5 ft.* Hit: 8 (1d6+5) slashing + 3 (1d6) poison. CON DC 14 or blinded until end of next turn.
**Spectral Wave (Phase 2, Recharge 5–6).** 20 ft. cone. DEX DC 15: 21 (6d6) radiant (half on success).

### Reactions
**Parry.** +3 AC against one melee attack (must see attacker, wielding melee weapon).

### Legendary Actions (3)
**Quick Dash.** Move up to speed without opportunity attacks.
**Scimitar Slice.** One scimitar attack.
**Command the Crew (2 Actions).** Ally within 30 ft. uses reaction to make one melee attack.
