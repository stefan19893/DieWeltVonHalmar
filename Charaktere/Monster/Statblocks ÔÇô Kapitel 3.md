## Johnas (Eingesperrte Seele)

*Im [[Kelch der Goldenen Gier]] gefangen*

*Medium undead, neutral evil* · **CR 12 (8.400 XP)**

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 17 (natural armor) | 126 (12d8+72) | 0 ft. (hovers within 5 ft. of cup) |

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 18 (+4) | 10 (+0) | 22 (+6) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 18 (+4) |

**Saving Throws:** Con +10, Cha +8
**Immunities (damage):** Poison, necrotic, psychic; bludg./pierc./slash. nonmagic
**Immunities (cond.):** Charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, prone
**Senses:** Blindsight 60 ft., passive Perception 11

**Gilded Essence.** Creatures starting turn within 10 ft. of cup: CON DC 16 or Restrained as skin turns gold (repeat save at turn end).
**Bound Soul.** Can't move >5 ft. from chalice. If chalice destroyed: banished to Nine Hells.

### Actions
**Multiattack.** Two Gilded Touch attacks.
**Gilded Touch.** *Melee +8, reach 5 ft.* Hit: 18 (4d6+4) necrotic + 7 (2d6) fire (molten gold).
**Golden Breath (Recharge 5–6).** 20 ft. cone. DEX DC 16: 45 (10d8) fire (half on success).
